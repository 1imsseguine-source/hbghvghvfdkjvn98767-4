7-Day Trip: Imperial Cities & Sahara Desert

Published on October 26, 2024 | Category: Imperial Cities & Desert

Experience the best of both worlds on this week-long adventure. Our popular 7 day trip through the imperial cities is perfect for a one week in Morocco experience, blending rich history with the breathtaking landscapes of the Sahara.

Full Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Fes

Welcome to Morocco! Arrive at Fes-Saïss airport and transfer to your traditional Riad in the heart of the Medina.

Day 2: Fes Sightseeing

Explore the spiritual and cultural capital of Morocco. Visit the Bou Inania Medersa, the tanneries, and the vibrant souks of Fes el-Bali.

Day 3: Fes to Merzouga Desert

Journey south through the Middle Atlas Mountains, passing through Ifrane and Azrou. Arrive at the edge of the Sahara in Merzouga for a sunset camel trek.

Day 4: Sahara Experience & Todra Gorges

Wake up to a desert sunrise. After breakfast, travel to the spectacular Todra Gorges, a massive trench carved by the river.

Day 5: Todra Gorges to Ouarzazate

Travel the "Road of a Thousand Kasbahs". Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Benhaddou, a famous filming location.

Day 6: Ouarzazate to Marrakech

Cross the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass, enjoying panoramic views on your way to the vibrant city of Marrakech.

Day 7: Marrakech & Departure

Explore the famous Djemaa el-Fna square and the Koutoubia Mosque. Depending on your flight, enjoy some last-minute souvenir shopping before transferring to the airport.
