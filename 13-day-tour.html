13-Day Tour: Grand Morocco from Casablanca

Published on October 18, 2024 | Category: Grand Tours

For the ultimate Moroccan experience, our 13-day tour from Casablanca covers all the highlights. From ancient medinas to vast deserts and beautiful coastlines, this is the definitive journey.

Full Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Casablanca

Arrive at Mohammed V Airport and transfer to your hotel. Visit the magnificent Hassan II Mosque.

Day 2: Casablanca to Chefchaouen

Travel to Rabat, the capital, and then continue to the famous "Blue City" of Chefchaouen in the Rif Mountains.

Day 3: Chefchaouen to Fes

Explore the blue-washed streets of Chefchaouen before traveling to the ancient Roman ruins of Volubilis and then on to Fes.

Day 4: Fes Guided Tour

Discover the intellectual and spiritual heart of Morocco with a full-day guided tour of Fes.

Day 5: Fes to Merzouga Desert

A long but scenic drive through the Atlas Mountains to the Sahara Desert.

Day 6: Sahara Desert Experience

Enjoy a full day in the desert, including a 4x4 excursion and a camel trek to your luxury desert camp.

Day 7: Merzouga to Dades Valley

Travel via the Todra Gorges to the beautiful Dades Valley.

Day 8: Dades Valley to Marrakech

Journey along the Road of a Thousand Kasbahs, visiting Ait Benhaddou en route to Marrakech.

Day 9: Marrakech Guided Tour

Explore the "Red City" with a local guide, visiting the Bahia Palace, Saadian Tombs, and Djemaa el-Fna.

Day 10: Marrakech Day Trip

Choose from our fantastic Morocco day tours, such as a trip to the Atlas Mountains or the coastal town of Essaouira.

Day 11: Marrakech to Essaouira

Travel to the charming coastal city of Essaouira, known for its relaxed vibe and fresh seafood.

Day 12: Essaouira to Casablanca

Enjoy a final Moroccan morning on the coast before driving back to Casablanca.

Day 13: Departure

Transfer to the airport for your departure, filled with unforgettable memories of Morocco.
