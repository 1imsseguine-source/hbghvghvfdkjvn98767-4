5-Day Tour: Marrakech to Fes via Sahara

Published on October 22, 2024 | Category: Desert Tours

This 5-day tour from Marrakech to Fes is one of our most popular desert adventures. It’s the perfect way to connect two of Morocco’s most iconic cities while experiencing the magic of the Sahara.

Full Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Marrakech to Dades Gorges

Depart from Marrakech and cross the High Atlas Mountains. Visit the Kasbah of Ait Benhaddou before continuing to the stunning Dades Gorges.

Day 2: Dades Gorges to Merzouga

Travel through the Valley of Roses and Todra Gorges. Arrive in Merzouga, where you will embark on a camel trek into the Erg Chebbi dunes for an overnight stay in a desert camp.

Day 3: Exploring the Merzouga Region

Spend a full day exploring the desert. Visit Khamlia village to listen to Gnawa music, meet nomad families, and enjoy the serene desert landscape.

Day 4: Merzouga to Midelt

After sunrise and breakfast, travel north along the Ziz Valley, known for its lush palm groves. Stop in Midelt, a town nestled in the Atlas Mountains.

Day 5: Midelt to Fes

Continue your journey to Fes, passing through the cedar forests of Azrou where you might see Barbary apes. Arrive in Fes in the late afternoon, concluding your tour.

Tour Details & Tips

What's Included:

  • Private 4x4 vehicle
  • English-speaking driver
  • Overnight in desert camp
  • Camel ride (one per person)
  • Half-board accommodation

What's Not Included:

  • Lunches
  • Drinks
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Entrance fees
